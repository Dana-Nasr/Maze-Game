Maze

Runner

The Maze Runner game is a fun maze game where you travel through different paths and try not to get lost until you reach your final destination!

Game's

Guide

Maze Runner is a simple game, you start moving from one place then you navigate through complicated pathways and collect starts through your journey, till you reach the end.

Start the journey

Hit the road and navigate your way through different paths.

Collect stars

Collect as much stars as you can, this will help you unlock new levels in the game.

Reach your destination

Don’t get lost in the maze and reach your destination safely.

Characters

You can choose to play the game through three different characters
